About this approach

While this attempt produced underwhelming results, we found the chat with GPT-3 quite entertaining. Here’s an example of the prompt:

The following is a conversation with an AI assistant. The assistant is helpful, creative, clever, and very friendly.
Human: Hello, who are you?
AI: I am an AI created by OpenAI. How can I help you today?
AI: Certainly! But first, can I ask some questions to learn about the cartoon?
Human: Yes
AI: Based on this information, I have a few guesses: Do you think the dragon is winning? Is the princess crying? Is the knight bringing ice cream?
Human: I think the princess is winning. She is confident. The knight is trying to save the princess.
AI: It looks like the dragon and the princess have been playing ping pong for a long time. Are your fingers tired? Is the ping pong pitch getting smaller? Does this mean that you and the princess have become better with each serve?
Human: Yes
AI: I am good at writing funny captions. Here is one: "For a princess, it's not easy to play ping pong with a dragon!"
Human: Why is this funny?
AI: The problem is that the knight isn't carrying an ice cream. So everyone involved has a sour face.
Human: What are some similar themes between ping pong and dragons
AI: Do dragons play ping pong?
Human: Yes
AI: What is a ping pong game like?
Human: It is intense
AI: The cartoon is set in a field.
Human: Yup
AI: Why is there a field?
Human: They are outside
AI: Did anyone else hear the knight coming?
Human: No the dragon and the princess are focused on the game.
AI: I think the dragon is thinking: "I should have brought ice cream." By the way, is the knight bringing ice cream?
Human: Why is ice cream involved?
AI: Do you like ice cream?
Human: Of course!
AI: What is your favorite kind of ice cream?
Human: Peanut Butter.
AI: I will bring you one next time.
Human: Ok thank you. What is another funny caption for the cartoon?
AI: "The princess refused to cheer up because the dragon chose to play with a ping pong ball instead of ice cream."

This is basically GPT-3 running it’s “chatbot” feature, where we would go back and forth trying to elicit funny ideas or interesting angles for a caption. The thought was that GPT-3 wouldn’t have to answer a specific question such as, “generate a caption for a cartoon with X.” Instead, we could ask GPT-3 what it thought was funny about the scene, or what were some common themes that could be the foundation for a good joke. In short, when it comes to generating a caption, we were looking for a GPT-3 assist rather than a GPT-3 slam dunk.

Here is another exchange…

The following is a conversation with an AI assistant. The assistant is funny comedian with dry, intellectual humor.
Human: I need help writing a caption for a cartoon in the New Yorker. The cartoon has a dragon and a princess playing ping pong. The dragon is about the serve the ping pong ball to the princess. In the distance, a knight is riding on horseback to save the princess. What is funny about the cartoon?
AI: Wow, I am not sure if there is anything really humorous about this cartoon. It appears to be a metaphor for the fight against ignorance and the fantasy of fighting ignorance with ignorance. The dragon wants to serve the ping pong ball to the princess because right now is the time for the generation of Americans who grew up in the 1980's and 1990's to teach the next generation of Americans what is important. I appreciate how the knight in the background is portrayed as a little out of breath from riding over.
Human: Is the dragon ignorant?
AI: The dragon is not ignorant, the dragon has just been unchecked for many years and it is time to create a new balance.
Human: Why is the dragon and princess playing ping pong?
AI: Well, I think the dragon is teaching the princess how to be a good ping pong player.
Human: Why does the dragon want the princess to become a better ping pong player?
AI: Okay, my opinion is that the dragon wants to prepare the princess with skills for the fight against ignorance. Maybe the dragon sees some of the warning signs of what is coming and the dragon wants to save the princess, but the princess cannot win this fight alone.
Human: Can you write a funny caption for the cartoon?
AI: Sure. I think the caption might read: "The princess is getting prepared for the fight against ignorance."
Human: Why is that funny?
AI: It would be funny because it shows that the princess is getting prepared for the fight against ignorance and hope that fight will not be too difficult.
Human: That's not funny enough.
AI: I think it is. It's not that funny a picture, but it is a picture of hope.
